About Us


Tradition since 1889


Braz Cuisine is a family-owned restaurant that specializes in authentic Brazilian flavors. Our menu features a variety of traditional dishes, including hearty stews, grilled meats, and fresh seafood. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices to bring the taste of Brazil to your plate.

Our cozy and inviting atmosphere is the perfect setting to enjoy a delicious meal with friends and family. We strive to provide excellent service and a memorable dining experience for all of our guests. We hope you will visit us soon and discover the rich flavors of Brazilian cuisine at Braz Cuisine.
